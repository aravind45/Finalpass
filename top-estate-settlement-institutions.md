# Top Financial Institutions for Estate Settlement Platform

## Tier 1: The Big Four (Highest Priority)

### 1. **Fidelity Investments** 
- **AUM:** $5.5 trillion (2024)
- **Estate Volume:** Estimated 150,000+ estates annually
- **Pain Points:** Notorious for slow estate processing (Reddit complaints)
- **Opportunity:** Largest individual investor base, high estate frequency
- **Integration Potential:** Has digital infrastructure, could be early adopter

### 2. **Vanguard Group**
- **AUM:** $10.1 trillion (2024) 
- **Estate Volume:** Estimated 120,000+ estates annually
- **Pain Points:** Manual processes, long wait times
- **Opportunity:** Index fund focus = high retail investor deaths
- **Integration Potential:** Conservative but efficiency-focused

### 3. **Charles Schwab**
- **AUM:** $7.5 trillion (2024)
- **Estate Volume:** Estimated 100,000+ estates annually
- **Pain Points:** Complex multi-platform estate handling
- **Opportunity:** Tech-forward company, likely open to innovation
- **Integration Potential:** High - already digitally advanced

### 4. **BlackRock (iShares)**
- **AUM:** $11.6 trillion (2024)
- **Estate Volume:** Estimated 80,000+ estates annually
- **Pain Points:** ETF estate transfers complex
- **Opportunity:** Largest asset manager globally
- **Integration Potential:** Corporate focus, but huge volume

## Platform Strategy by Tier

### Phase 1: Prove Concept (Months 1-3)
**Target:** Invesco + 1-2 smaller firms
- Build credibility with documented case studies
- Focus on clear pain points and measurable improvements

### Phase 2: Scale to Big Four (Months 4-12)
**Target:** Fidelity, Schwab, Vanguard
- Use Invesco success stories as proof points
- Emphasize efficiency gains and customer satisfaction

### Phase 3: Enterprise Expansion (Year 2)
**Target:** JPMorgan, BlackRock, Bank of America
- Full API integrations
- White-label solutions
- Revenue sharing partnerships

## Key Success Factors

### 1. **Start with Pain Points**
Focus on institutions with documented customer complaints (Fidelity, Invesco)

### 2. **Leverage Network Effects**
Once you have 2-3 major institutions, others will follow to stay competitive

### 3. **Build for Scale**
Design platform to handle multiple institution requirements simultaneously

### 4. **Focus on ROI**
Each institution saves $200-500 per estate in processing costs

The key insight: **Fidelity should be your primary target after Invesco** - they have the most documented customer pain points and the largest retail investor base, making them the highest-volume opportunity for estate settlements.
